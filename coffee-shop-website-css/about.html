More Than Coffee — A Community Cup

Brew & Bean was founded in 2010 by two friends who believed that great coffee should bring people together. What started as a tiny corner shop has grown into a neighbourhood staple, but our mission has never changed: source ethically, roast carefully, and serve with warmth.

Every bean we use is single-origin and purchased directly from family-run farms. We roast in small batches every week so that what lands in your cup is always fresh. Our baristas train for months before they ever pull a shot — because we think the craft matters as much as the bean.

Whether you're stopping in for a quick espresso before work or settling in for an afternoon with your laptop, you're always welcome here.
